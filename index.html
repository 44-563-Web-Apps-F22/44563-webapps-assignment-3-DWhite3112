Nelson Atkins

The Nelson Atkins Museum has a vast collection of art pieces. The Japanese collection really draws my eyes to it. My favorite piece in this collection being the Striding Lion: Mount for the Buddhist Deity Monju, Mid-13th century. Japanese. The design of the lion has a strong and intimidating aura to it, that's what I think makes it a great piece.

Directions from MCI to Nelson Atkins

The closest airport to Nelson Atkins would be the Kansas City International airport. To get from the airport you will need to:

  1. Take I-29 S/US-71 S in May Township from NW 120th St
  2. Continue on US-71 to Kaw Township. Take the exit toward Emanuel Cleaver II Blvd/Swope Pkwy from US-71 S
  3. Take Emanuel Cleaver II Blvd. to Oak St
  4. The museum will be at 4525 Oak St, Kansas City, MO 64111

There are plenty of other good places to visit around the museum such as:
  • The Ewing and Muriel Kauffman Memorial Garden
  • Manheim Park + Garden
  • The National Museum of Toys and Miniatures
  • Loose Park Garden Center
